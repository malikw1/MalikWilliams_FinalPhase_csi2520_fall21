Ready Recipes

Quick recipes, ready for you!

Breakfast:

Scrambled eggs: Need: 1. eggs 2. whisk 3. bowl 4. skillet --

1. crack two eggs in a bowl 2. stir the eggs with the whisk 3. add any seasoning you desire 4. put the eggs in the skillet on the stove at desired temperature 5. scramble eggs until completion

Pancakes: Need: 1. pancake mix 2. bowl 3. spoon 4. skillet 5. water --

1. add pancake mix mix and water to your bowl 2. stir the mix and water thoroughly 3. spread mix into skillet evenly with desired temperature 4. Wait for bubbles in pancake and flip when ready

Hash Browns: Need: 1. hash browns 2. cooking oil 3. skillet --

1. heat up cooking oil to desired temperature in skillet 2. add hashbrowns into oil 3. remove hashbrowns when brown and ready

Lunch:

Grilled Cheese: Need: 1. bread 2. cheese 3. butter 4. skillet 5. spatula --

1. apply butter to each side of your bread 2. place bread into the skillet on the stove at your desired temperature 3. flip bread when brown 4. apply a slide of cheese between two slices of bread

French Fries: Need: 1. cooking oil 2. french fries 3. skillet 4. spoon --

1. heat up cooking oil on stove at desire temperature 2. apply french fries for desired amount of time until brown 3. remove fries when ready

Salad: Need: 1. lettuce 2. cheese. 3. tomatoes 4. croutons 5. salad dressing --

1. add lettuce into a bowl 2. add desired amount of vegetables into bowl 3. apply salad dressing into bowl 4. enjoy

Dinner:

Burger: Need: 1. beef patties 2. burger buns 3. cheese 4. lettuce 5. tomatoes --

1. heat up skillet to desired temperature 2. season patty as desired 3. add beef patty to skillet 4. flip patty until done 5. add patty between buns 6. add desired toppings to the burger

Pizza: Need: 1. frozen pizza 2. pizza pan --

1. preheat oven to desired temperature 2. place froze pizz on pan and into the oven 3. remove the pizza when done

Grilled Chicken sandwich Need: Need: 1. chicken patties 2. burger buns 3. cheese 4. lettuce 5. tomatoes --

1. preheat oven to desired temperature 2. season chicken as desired 3. place patty in overlay 4. flip patty until done 5. remove patty when done 6. place patty between buns 7. add cheese and desire toppings to sandwich
